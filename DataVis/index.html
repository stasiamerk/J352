Navient Student Loan Complaints From the Past Year

By: Anastasia Merkulova

Navient Corporation is one of the nationâ€™s largest federal student loan servicers, according to an article from Student Loan Planner . The article states that the corporation has been involved in a growing number of lawsuits from the Consumer Financial Protection Bureau, state attorneys generals and teachers since 2017. A major settlement that requires Navient to provide $1.85 billion in relief to private and federal student loan borrowers was announced in January of 2022, the article says.

There have been 88 complaints involving student loans made against Navient Corporation in the past year, according to the Consumer Complaint Database . 51 of these complaints were about dealing with a lender or servicer, 28 were about struggling to repay the loan, one was about incorrect report information and the last one was about an issue with the income share agreement, according to the database.

The month with the most complaints (22) in the past year was September 2024. This was also the month with the most complaints made in the past three years. According to the data from the website, April 2025 was the first month in the past three years with zero complaints made.
